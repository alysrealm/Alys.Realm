What is "The Realm"?

This is my personal website! I knew as soon as I started exploring the world of the personal web, that I wanted to join in! To make my own little cyberspace/ art project/ collection of the things I love. Welcome to my realm! The residents are faeries, goblins, demons, myths, precious woodland animals, and even a colony of feral cats! From spooky forests to tranquil gardens, there's certain to be cute, weird, gothic, artsy, fun, and whimsical things! and also probably some chaos...

Who's Alys?

Alys (ALICE) is me; aka Alysson. Or Syla if you're funky. I'm the hobbyist-developer, webmistress of this site... I'm a 21 yr old monster-in-girl-form living in rural Northern Appalachia. I survived a terminal diagnosis which left me disabled. MORE IMPORTANTLY THOUGH! I am an artist, animal lover, collector, nerd, multi-genre music lover, reader, dreamer, appreciator of cute things, heathen, crafter, lover of learning and a wannabe pixie.
