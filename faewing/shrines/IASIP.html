What is "Always Sunny in Philadelphia?

Always Sunny is an American sitcom created by Rob McElhenney with Glenn Howerton. The show features 'The Gang', a.k.a. Mac, Charlie, Dennis, Dee, and eventually Frank; a group of friends who are so depraved and degenerate that they end up in the wackiest situations. They own a dive bar in South Philadelphia, called Paddy's Pub, but spend a majority of their time up to shenanigans, leaving their bar quite barren as far as customers go.
